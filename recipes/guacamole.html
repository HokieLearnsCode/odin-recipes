Guacamole Recipe:

About This Guacamole

It's FREAKING Guacamole!

Cooking Tools and Ingredients For Guacamole

  • Potato Masher
  • Medium serving bowl
  • Chopping board
  • Knife for chopping onion
  • 2 Ripe avocados
  • 1 Small onion
  • 1 lime cut in 2 halves
  • 1 clove of minced garlic
  • Salt and pepper
  • Red pepper flakes

Instructions for making your guacamole

  1. Cut open your avocados, throw away the pits and scoop out the mesocarp (the part people eat) and toss it in to the serving bowl
  2. Now that the avocado is in the bowl start mashing away
  3. Chop your onion up in to nice small pieces
  4. In the bowl, mix in your onion and garlic
  5. Squeeze the juice of the lime in to the bowl
  6. Add the salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes to your personal tastes
  7. Mix everything together
  8. Cover and rest for 30 minutes
  9. Enjoy with chips or some delicious tacos!
